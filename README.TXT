PROJECT TITLE: Scrabble 
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: To create a text based scrabble game for 1 to 4 players. 
AUTHORS:Seb Ezman, Jeronimo Cumming , Simon Damato , Kaitlyn Conron
USER INSTRUCTIONS: To move input: x y direction word. x y = x y coordinates of word start tile, direction = R or D (right or down), word = word you want to play for. The first move  must begin on the coordinates 8 8.
